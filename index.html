Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug receiving the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970

Dr. Norman Borlaug (1914-2009) was an American agronomist who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. Borlaug was awarded multiple honors for his work, including the Nobel Peace Prize, the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and the Congressional Gold Medal.
